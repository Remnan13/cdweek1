The Imperial Summer Palace

Beauty in the Shadow of War

A place for relaxation and reflection

Seat of dynastic kings of the Korean (Goryeo) peninsula, there is a quiet beauty to behold while strolling the gravel and stone paths which weave through the sprawling palace grounds.

The structures appear to be well restored, but the grounds have been incinerated by Japanese invasions several times in Korea's history.
